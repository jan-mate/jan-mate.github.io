Misophonia Sounds

What is this?

Misophonia is a disorder characterized by an intense emotional reaction, such as anger or disgust, to specific sounds, often those made by other people, like chewing or breathing.

Pick the sounds you want included, set how often they play and how unpredictable the timing is, then press the big play button.
